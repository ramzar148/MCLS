## ğŸ†˜ Common Questions

**Q: I don't see any calls on my dashboard**
A: This means no calls have been logged in your region yet. Check that:
- Your region is correctly configured
- Filters are not applied (click Reset)

**Q: I see calls from other regions**
A: This shouldn't happen. Contact system administrator if this occurs.

**Q: Can I create a maintenance call?**
A: No, but you can instruct department officials to log calls via the system. Any authenticated user can log calls.

**Q: Why can't I assign technicians?**
A: This is a Facilities Manager function. Your role is monitoring and oversight.

**Q: How do I change my email notifications?**
A: Contact system administrator to update your notification preferences.

**Q: Can I export call data?**
A: Not yet - this feature is planned for future release.
